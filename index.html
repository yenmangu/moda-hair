About Moda

Robert Chaumon started cutting hair over 30 years ago,  opening his first hair salon in the heart of Chelmsford. Thousands of happy clients later and now Moda Hair and Beauty Salon in Essex is quickly became the go-to hairdressers in Chelmsford for the area’s many executives and professionals.

We believe that a visit to our hair salon should be more than just a haircut or colour. We endeavour to offer the best hairdressing experience and service in Chelmsford, focussed on the longevity and health of your hair.

Our luxury Hair Services

At Moda, we create an environment where you feel comfortable to be yourself. From the moment you arrive, until the moment you leave, we strive to give you the best possible experience. Most importantly, we care about the health of your hair. That will be always be our priority, no matter what. Healthy hair is better than any colour.
